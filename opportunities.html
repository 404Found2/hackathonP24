Income Earning Categories

Bonds/CDs


Bond and CDs, or certificates of deposits, can be excellent ways to steadily increase your profits. Because they do not fluctuate based upon the economy and there is a very little risk involved, this investment can be used to diversify your portfolio.

Stocks


Although riskier than Bonds or CDs, stocks have a greater return on investment and are one of the most common ways to save money. Stocks generally are more passive ways to generate money as they are more long term investments.

Real Estate


Although real estate often requires more liquid cash, its potential return on investment is also greater. Great real estate can generate extra cash flow. Real estate also comes with its costs: property taxes, rent evasion, vacancy, damages to property etc.

Royalties


While royalties require less startup capital they tend to require more time and energy in making the content-creator known. In addition, partnering companies usually take the majority of profits, thus reducing the amount the content creator earns.

Small Business/ Entrepreneurship


Creating your own business can be a fun way to monetize your passion and discover the thrill of being your own boss. Although experience and a great business model is required, there is a lot of potential growth in this field.

Precious Metals/ Currencies


As gold, silver, and other commodities increase over time, they can be great ways to preserve wealth. In addition, since this item consists of tangible goods, there is limited risks involved. Currencies work similarly.

Part-Time Jobs


A Part-Time Job can be a consistent way to earn extra money to meet your financial goals. Getting small jobs such as being an Uber driver or working at a local restaurant can be rewarding and something worth considering as a part time.
